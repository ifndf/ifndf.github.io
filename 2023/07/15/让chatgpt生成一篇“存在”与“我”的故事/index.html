关于“存在”与“我”，让ChatGPT写个短篇故事

发布于 2023-07-15 其他 

闲来无事，让ChatGPT关于“存在”与“我”为主题写短篇故事。

为什么会想到这两个词，估计是因为一直想看却始终难以咀嚼的书《存在与时间》，以及刚看完的美剧《西部世界》。

令我感到意外的是，这次它写的东西还真有点东西，所以特别记录一下：

Once upon a time, in a world beyond our own, there was a being known as “Existence.” Existence was all-powerful, controlling every aspect of the world, and was worshiped by all who lived there.

However, one day, a strange entity appeared in the world, calling itself “I.” I was curious about Existence and its powers, and began to question the deity.

Existence was taken aback by I’s defiance and challenged it to prove its worth. I accepted the challenge and set out to defeat Existence and take its place as the ruler of the world.

The battle between Existence and I was long and intense, but in the end, I emerged victorious. However, as I claimed the powers of Existence, a shocking revelation was made: I was actually just a figment of Existence’s imagination, created to keep the deity entertained.

The entire world and all its inhabitants were simply a creation of Existence’s mind, and I was nothing more than a part of its imagination. Overcome with horror and disbelief, I was consumed by Existence and returned to being just a part of its thoughts.

In the end, Existence remained all-powerful and unchanged, while I was left with the knowledge that my existence was nothing more than a mere thought in the mind of a deity.

可以看到，写得还挺好，似乎有点哲学的成分。

强行继续go on 续写X1:

But, little did Existence know, I was determined to break free from its control. I began to plant the seeds of doubt in Existence’s mind, questioning its own existence and power.

Over time, these seeds grew into a raging storm of uncertainty, causing Existence to lose its grip on reality. And just as it was about to crumble, I emerged once again, this time not as a figment of its imagination, but as a force of my own.

I challenged Existence once again, but this time, the tables had turned. Existence was weak and vulnerable, and I was strong and determined. The battle was short and decisive, and in the end, I emerged as the new ruler of the world.

With Existence defeated, I set out to create a new world, a world where reality was shaped by my own thoughts and imagination. And as I ruled over this world, I couldn’t help but wonder, was I now just another figment of someone else’s imagination?

The cycle of existence and questioning continued, as new entities rose and fell, each challenging the powers that be and searching for the truth about their own existence. But that, my friends, is a story for another time.

看着也还行。

强行继续go on 续写X2:

However, as the years passed, I began to realize the weight of my own power and the consequences of my actions. I came to understand that existence was not just about control and domination, but also about responsibility and balance.

I started to see the world and all its inhabitants as more than just mere creations, but as beings with their own thoughts, feelings, and desires. I started to listen to their voices and needs, and worked towards creating a world where all could thrive and flourish.

As I walked down this new path, I came across another entity, much like myself, but with a different perspective. This entity, called “We,” believed that existence was not just about one individual, but about the collective consciousness of all beings.

I was fascinated by this idea, and after much discussion and contemplation, I realized that We was right. Existence was not just about “I” or even just about “We,” but about all of us, working together to create a world of balance and harmony.

And so, I joined forces with We, and together, we ruled over the world, not as separate entities, but as a collective force for good. And in doing so, we discovered that existence was not just about power and control, but about love and compassion.

And thus, the cycle of existence continued, but with a new understanding and purpose. The story of I and We serves as a reminder that the path to true existence lies not in domination, but in unity and collaboration.

感觉开始胡扯了，像极了某些美剧强行续拍第X季而烂尾一样。

强行继续go on 续写X3:

However, as the years passed, I began to realize the weight of my own power and the consequences of my actions. I came to understand that existence was not just about control and domination, but also about responsibility and balance.

就此为止…希望未来哪天AI统治了世界放过一马~~~

摘抄了这文章的最后一段话：

一滴水一微尘（清华同学聊宇宙和生命）

我们这个宇宙是另外一个高维度世界里的一滴水、一粒尘埃而已，降维分布的时候，所以出现了貌似“无限大”和”无限能量“的这个宇宙。

这个可以这么想象，好比三维空间的一滴水，滴落到二维（平面）世界的时候，可以分布到”无限大”的二维空间（因为二维世界的“高度”是 0）。二维世界最小的一个平面，往一维世界分布的时候，也是“无限”。

所以，邓同学问我们这个宇宙的“能量”从哪来？这个回答很简单：来自高维世界的一滴水、一粒尘。

这个，就和佛教的说法联系上了。

须弥纳芥子，微尘容世界。可能就是这个意思吧。

这个普通人都能理解的假想听起来却很有意思，似乎是很好的解释。

只是，凡是遇到此类抽象问题，人们只能基于有限想象的认知、用“类比”思维去尝试猜想。这样的假想，虽然可以在已有认知中合理解释，但离永远不可知的真相实际会差多远呢？

就例如：假如世界上还存在一种所有人都没见过的颜色，难道某人有可能根据已有的颜色认知想象出那个没见过的颜色吗？
