浏览器插件

强烈推荐一个浏览器扩展插件：Saladict 沙拉查词 (crimx.com)

遇到英文词汇，双击一下就弹出中文释义了。

ChatGPT

ChatGPT 的出现，其实是个人高效学习英语的最大利器。因为当它用英语回答时，不可能出现语法错误、拼写错误、表达不准确等等这些低级的Chinglish问题。

利用它学习英语，关键在于提问技巧、连续提问以及自身的专注耐心。

例如，可以结合Rachel老师所说的：

  • 看到日常事物/画面时，用脑子头脑风暴一下当前事物中包含哪些名词Nouns、动词Verbs，以及形容词Adjustives 。

    因为只要有这三要素，就能形成一个句子、简洁准确地描述一件事了.

  • 由于这些词汇都是日常相关的，因此能每时每刻不经意地高效联想记忆。

由于我们只是水平有限的人类，所以“头脑风暴”这种事情就可以交给ChatGPT了。

我想到可以用这样的句子是提问ChatGPT:

please list all of nouns,verbs,adjectives and sentences in English about .

举个例子，比如我刚洗完澡，看到一个吹风机，我可以这样问ChatGPT:

Please list all of nouns,verbs,adjustives and sentences in English about “吹风机”.

AI回复以下：

Nouns:

  • Hair dryer
  • Blow dryer
  • Hair blower
  • Hot air blower
  • Styling tool
  • Appliance
  • Machine
  • Cord
  • Nozzle
  • Fan

Verbs:

  • Blow
  • Dry
  • Style
  • Heat
  • Control
  • Adjust
  • Plug in
  • Turn on/off
  • Hold
  • Point
  • Direct
  • Switch

Adjectives:

  • Hot
  • Cold
  • High
  • Low
  • Powerful
  • Adjustable
  • Portable
  • Electric
  • Stylish
  • Modern

Sentences:

  • I need to buy a new hair dryer because my old one broke.
  • My hair dryer has a cold air setting that I use to set my hairstyle.
  • A hair blower is a great tool for drying and styling your hair.
  • The hot air blower is too hot for my scalp, I need to use the cool setting.
  • I use my hair dryer every morning to dry and style my hair.
  • This hair dryer has a lot of power and can dry my hair quickly.
  • The adjustable nozzle on my hair dryer allows me to control the direction of the airflow.
  • I always bring my portable hair dryer with me when I travel.
  • The cord on my hair dryer is too short, I need to use an extension cord.
  • When using a hair dryer, it’s important to hold it a few inches away from your hair to avoid damaging it.

若在ChatGPT列出答案后，仍有不认识的词汇：

  • 要么利用浏览器插件沙拉查词功能、直接双击查看意思；

  • 要么继续提问ChatGPT词汇意思，无限扩展延申，加强理解记忆更多内容
