印象深刻的高中英语试卷文章《reason,season and lifetime》

发布于 2023-01-15

People always come into your life: for a reason,a season,and a lifetime.When you figure out which it is, you know exactly what to do.

When someone is in your life for a reason,it is usually to meet a need you have expressed outwardly or inwardly.They have come to assist you through a difficulty,or to provide you with guidence and support,to aid you physically,emotionally,or even spiritually.They may seem like a god sent to you,and they are.They are there for the reason you need them to be.

When people come into your life for a season,it is because your turn has come to share,grow or learn.They may bring you an experience of peace or make you laugh. They may teach you something you have never done.They usually give you an unbelievable amount of joy.Believe it.It is real.But it is only for a season.And like spring turns to summer and summer to fall,tne season eventually ends.

Lifetime relationships teach you a lifetime of lessons–those things you must build upon,in order to have a solid emotional foundation and put what you have learned to use in all other relationships and ares of your life.
